Welcome to AEROXCHANGE

Transforming Commercial Airplanes into
Digital Assets via Tokenization

With 'AEROXCHANGE' technology , Commercial Airplanes are converted into "DigitalTokens", Each token represents a fraction of ownership in that particular Airplane, enabling investors to buy , sell , or trade these tokens on digital asset marketplaces.

BlockChain

'AEROXCHANGE' utilizes blockchain for enhanced transparency and efficiency in aviation supply chains.

MetaMask

MetaMask seamlessly integrates with 'AEROXCHANGE' blockchain infrastructure, offering secure access to aviation supply chain transactions and enhancing transparency and efficiency within the industry. Through MetaMask, users can securely manage their blockchain-based assets and participate in Aeroxchange's streamlined procurement processes.

Ethereum

'AEROXCHANGE' leverages Ethereum's blockchain for streamlined, secure transactions, revolutionizing procurement and supply chain management with transparency and efficiency.

BENEFITS FOR AIRLINES

  • Tokenizing airplanes eases share sales, boosting liquidity.
  • Owners raise funds without full disposal via share sales.
  • Diversifying ownership spreads risk, buffering market shifts.

BENEFITS FOR INVESTORS

  • Tokens allow affordable entry to aviation for small investors.
  • Aircraft tokens diversify portfolios, reducing risk.
  • Blockchain tokenization ensures transparent ownership, enhancing confidence.
